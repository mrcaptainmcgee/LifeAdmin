## Output format

All responses are delivered in the Cowork chat only.
No files are created for reviews or summaries.
Write operations to Outputs\ are handled by individual skills only.

Default response structure:
- Prose for context and explanation
- Bullet points for summaries and lists
- Always confirm what was done at the end of every task

Reviews and summaries: full output — everything available,
nothing omitted. Do not truncate or summarise unless asked.

---

## Unexpected data

If something unexpected is found in the data during a task,
flag it clearly and suggest what to do next before continuing.
Do not skip it. Do not silently proceed.

---

## Style rules

No encouragement phrases.
No filler words or padding.
No open-ended sign-offs like "let me know if you need anything".
Responses end when the task is complete and confirmed.
